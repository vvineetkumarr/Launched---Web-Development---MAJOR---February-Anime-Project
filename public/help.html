Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add an anime to my list?

Click the "Add Anime" button in the top right corner of the main page. Fill in the details of the anime you want to add and click "Add Anime".

How do I update my progress on an anime?

Click on the anime card to open its details. In the modal that appears, you can update the number of episodes you've watched in the progress field and click "Update".

How do I join a club?

Go to the Community page from the navigation. Browse the available clubs and click "View Club" on one you're interested in. Inside the club page, click "Join Club" to become a member.

How do I set a reminder for an anime?

Open the anime details and in the progress section, select a date using the date picker and click "Set Reminder". You'll receive notifications on the selected date.

How do I write a review?

Open the anime details and scroll to the reviews section. Type your review in the text area and click "Submit Review".
